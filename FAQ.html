Frequently Asked Question

I. Booking & Payment 💳

How far in advance should I book my yacht charter?

We strongly recommend booking **3 to 6 months in advance,** especially for peak seasons (summer) and major holidays, to ensure the widest selection of yachts and your preferred dates.

What is included in the base charter price?

The base price typically includes the rental of the yacht, the professional crew (Captain, Chef), standard water sports equipment, and linens/towels.

What is the *Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA)*?

The APA is a standard sum (usually **25%–35% of the base charter fee)** paid in advance for variable operating expenses, such as fuel, food, beverages, and dockage fees. It is managed by the Captain and reconciled at the end of the trip.

🛳️ II. Charter Details & Yacht Life

What qualifications does the crew have?

All captains hold internationally recognized maritime licenses (like USCG or MCA Master) and are highly experienced in their operating regions. The entire crew is trained in safety procedures, first aid, and hospitality standards..

Are children allowed on board?

Yes, children are welcome! We require life jackets to be worn by children under a certain age while underway. We will ask for the ages of all guests during booking to ensure the yacht and crew are appropriate for your family.

Can we customize the itinerary?

Absolutely. Your itinerary is fully customizable. While the Captain provides guidance based on weather, safety, and local knowledge, the route is flexible. We work with you pre-trip to draft a dream itinerary..

⚙️ III. Safety & Operations

What happens if the weather is bad?

The Captain will always prioritize guest safety. If weather prevents travel, the Captain will suggest alternative, safe itineraries in sheltered waters. If a trip is canceled before departure due to severe weather, specific refund/rescheduling policies apply per the charter agreement.

Is smoking allowed on the yacht?

YSmoking is generally not permitted inside the yacht cabin or staterooms due to safety and fabric damage risks. Smoking is usually allowed only in designated exterior areas, subject to the Captain's discretion..

Do I need to tip the crew?

Crew gratuity is customary in the yachting industry and reflects your satisfaction with the service. A typical gratuity for excellent service is 15% to 20% of the base charter fee, paid to the Captain at the end of the trip to be split among the crew.

🍽️ IV. Food & Beverages

How does the menu customization work?

Before your trip, you will complete a Preference Sheet detailing your dietary restrictions, allergies, favorite cuisines, and desired beverages. The onboard chef designs the menu specifically around your preferences.

Can we bring our own alcohol?

This varies by yacht type and location. For crewed charters, most food and beverage costs are covered by the APA, including alcohol purchased by the crew based on your preferences. Please inquire with your charter broker if you wish to bring specific items.
