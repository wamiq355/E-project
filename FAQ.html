Frequently Asked Question

I. Booking & Payment 💳

How far in advance should I book my yacht charter?

We strongly recommend booking **3 to 6 months in advance,** especially for peak seasons (summer) and major holidays, to ensure the widest selection of yachts and your preferred dates.

What is included in the base charter price?

The base price typically includes the rental of the yacht, the professional crew (Captain, Chef), standard water sports equipment, and linens/towels.

What is the *Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA)*?

The APA is a standard sum (usually **25%–35% of the base charter fee)** paid in advance for variable operating expenses, such as fuel, food, beverages, and dockage fees. It is managed by the Captain and reconciled at the end of the trip.
